How to Use PickleQ

A simple guide for running open play sessions

What is PickleQ?

‚ú® PickleQ helps you run fair pickleball open play at your court.

  • Players check in when they arrive
  • The app picks who plays next (fairly!)
  • You tap who won each game
  • Everyone sees their stats at the end

Before You Start

1

Open the App

Go to pickleq.app on your tablet or phone.

Works best on a tablet at the front desk!

2

Set Up Your Venue (One Time Only)

Tap the gear icon in the top corner.

Enter your venue name (like "Manila Pickleball Club").

This lets players see their stats online later!

Tip: You only need to do this once. The app remembers it.

Running a Session

1

Set Up the Session

Pick where you're playing (like "Courts A-D").

Set how many courts you have.

You need at least 4 players to start!
2

Add Players

Type each player's name and tap Add.

You can also pick their skill level (stars):

  • ‚òÖ = Beginner
  • ‚òÖ‚òÖ = Intermediate
  • ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ = Advanced
Tip: Skill levels help make fair teams!
3

Check Players In

Tap a player's name to check them in.

Only checked-in players will be put on courts.

Players can check out if they need a break!

4

Start Playing!

Tap the big green Start Session button.

The app will automatically put 4 players on each court.

The app picks players who played the least games first. This keeps it fair!
5

Record Winners

When a game ends, find that court on screen.

Tap the winning team (Blue or Orange side).

Those 4 players go back in line, and 4 new players get on court!

Made a mistake? A popup appears for 10 seconds to undo.
6

End the Session

When you're done, tap End Session.

You'll see the final leaderboard with everyone's stats!

Common Questions

What if a player arrives late?

Just add them and check them in! They'll join the next available game.

What if a player needs to leave early?

Tap their name and check them out. They won't be assigned to new games.

What if someone leaves mid-game?

The app will find a substitute from the waiting line!

Do I need internet?

The app works offline! Stats sync to the cloud when you have internet.

What if I close the app by accident?

Don't worry! The app saves everything. Just open it again.

How does the app pick teams?

It tries to balance skill levels. A strong player pairs with a newer player, so games are fair and fun!
